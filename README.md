# Cats vs. Rats: Plague Wars
You are a witch during a period when the plague is running rampant throughout the village. Rats are the prime suspect for the outbreak, and something must be done. Luckily, you house a multitude of fearless cats in your chamber. They keep out the ones they can, but as the rat population grows and mutates, you must take more extreme measures.  

You start to develop weapons that use your cats as ammo to terminate the pest population. Using the resources you have from the forest and your home; how long can you survive before the plague takes you?
